Project Name: AdForge AI - Automated Multi-Platform Copy System
Empathy Map Blueprint
Target User Persona: Sarah (Small Business Owner & Digital Marketer)
Core Problem Addressed: The exhausting, time-consuming process of manually writing, formatting, and adjusting marketing copy to meet strict, varying platform constraints (Instagram hashtags, Google text headers, Facebook body styles).

Says

  • "I waste hours rewriting and re-formatting the exact same ad pitch over and over just to fit Instagram, Facebook, and Google Ads guidelines."
  • "I need a single platform that can instantly generate high-converting copy pre-loaded with styled hashtag pills and contextual emojis."
  • "Hiring an agency or professional copywriter is way too expensive for my budget."

Thinks

  • "Will this AI platform actually understand my specific brand tone, or will it sound completely generic and robotic?"
  • "If I can rapidly test multiple copy variations per campaign, my click-through rates (CTR) and conversions should skyrocket."
  • "I hope this system automates my workflows enough so I can focus on scaling up actual product operations instead of writing text panels."

Does

  • Manually switches across multiple open browser tabs to test character constraints and format spacing layout boxes.
  • Copies, pastes, and re-edits previous high-performing ad briefs to use as raw template blocks for new platform submissions.
  • Spends weekends brainstorming fresh hooks, engaging body captions, and digging up optimized industry hashtags.

Feels

  • Overwhelmed by the relentless daily demand to pump out fresh creative copy variants to avoid campaign ad-fatigue.
  • Anxious about burning limited marketing spend on unoptimized, unengaging copy lines that fail to convert traffic.
  • Relieved and Empowered when a smart full-stack generator engine renders multi-platform options on a single dark-themed workspace click.
Empathy Map Matrix Breakdown

1. SAYS (Explicit Needs)

  • Expresses frustration with cross-platform formatting rules, character counts, and layout constraints.
  • Demands an easy-to-use user interface that delivers structured outputs natively rather than basic blocks of unparsed plain text.

3. DOES (Observed Behaviors)

  • Sifts through old documents and templates to stitch together working copy layouts by hand.
  • Spends excessive administrative hours preparing textual assets instead of designing high-level campaign strategies.

2. THINKS (Internal Motivations)

  • Wonders how to safely scale marketing volume without diluting the unique identity or tone of the brand.
  • Values rapid iteration capabilities, wanting to experiment with multiple visual variants (Professional, Luxury, Playful tones) instantly.

4. FEELS (Emotional Drivers)

  • Stressed about the risk of wasting capital on low-converting ad campaigns.
  • Excited about the potential of leveraging localized AI systems (like the LLaMA 3.3 production mirror) to eliminate copywriting bottlenecks.
